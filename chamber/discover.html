Eclipse

On April 7th, 2024, Morrilton Arkansas will be on the line of totality for a total Solar Eclipse. Make sure to book a place to stay for the chance to see this amazing phenomenon.

Petit Jean

With Arkansas's first state park just 15 minutes away from Morrilton, Petit Jean State Park is a great place to visit! Enjoy tons of opportunities to explore hiking trails, fish on beautiful lakes, and enjoy some time in the great outdoors.

Sites to See

Morrilton is surrounded fun places to explore. The Museum of Automobiles features cars made in 1904, a gun exhibit, and cars driven by Elvis Presley and President John F. Kennedy. With such a wide variety, there's something for everyone!

Dining

With a wide variety of food including mexican, steakhouses, and southern BBQ, there's a place for any food craving!

Things to Do

Only 45 minutes away from Morrilton is the capital of Arkansas, Little Rock. Visit sites like Pinnacle Mountain State Park, the Museum of Discovery, and the Clinton Presdential Library.
